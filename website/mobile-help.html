Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create my first event?
    To create your first event, log into your OnTimely dashboard and click the "Create Event" button. You'll be guided through a step-by-step process to set up your event details, guest list, and custom homepage.
  • How do I invite guests to my event?
    You can invite guests by uploading a CSV file with their email addresses, or by manually adding them one by one. Once added, you can send them custom invitation emails with your event homepage link.
  • Can I customize the event homepage?
    Yes! OnTimely offers extensive customization options including your logo, colors, background images, and content layout. You can create a unique branded experience for each event.
  • How does real-time communication work?
    Our real-time communication system allows you to send instant updates, announcements, and messages to all your guests. They'll receive notifications on their mobile devices and can respond in real-time.
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.
  • Is there a mobile app for guests?
    Yes! Guests can access your event through our mobile app available on iOS and Android. They can view event details, receive updates, and communicate with other guests.
